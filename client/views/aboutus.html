About Us

Our History

Started in 1814, originally named as Wagon Paint Shop, CPS quickly specialized into making astonishing paint jobs. The bright colors company used were not so popular but truely loyal customers remained and kept the company alive year after year. The transition from wagons to cars did not change this picture. The company is devoted to its roots and still paints car using methods and colors derived from ancestor.

You better paint your wagon than let it rot.

Horatio Wheelsaxel, The sayings of the master preserved to generations to come, Wagon Books, 1864
